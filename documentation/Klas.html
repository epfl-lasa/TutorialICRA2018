Klas Kronander received his M.S. degree in Applied Physics and Electrical Engineering from Linkoping Institute of Technology, Sweden, in 2010 and his Ph.D. in Robotics from Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne, Switzerland, in 2015. His research interests focus on robot control, manipulation and human-robot interaction. He was a postdoctoral researcher, and has consulted for various companies interested in compliant control and learning from demonstration. He is currently a Senior Roboticist at Vicarious AI.
