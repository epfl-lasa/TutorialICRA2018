Seyed Sina Mirrazavi Salehian is a Postdoctoral researcher at the Learning Algorithms and Systems Laboratory (LASA) at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL). He received his B.Sc(2010) and M.Sc (2012) Mechanical engineering from AmirKabir University of Technology and he recently received his Ph.D. in Robotics from the Swiss Federale Insitute of Technology in Lausanne, 2018. His research interests focus on robot control, multi-arm manipulations and human-robot interaction.
